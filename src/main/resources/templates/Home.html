"Keep the disc flying"

Ultimate Frisbee is a team sport played with a flying disc, combining elements of soccer, football, and basketball. Players aim to score points by passing the disc to teammates and advancing it to the opposing team's end zone. It's a non-contact and self-officiated game that values sportsmanship. "ULTIMATENAV" likely refers to a digital platform for Ultimate Frisbee, organized by KPT CAKRAWALA, facilitating event registration for players. ULTIMATENAV streamlines the process, allowing players to easily sign up for any events..
